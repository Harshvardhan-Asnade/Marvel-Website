Iron Man

Robert Downey Jr. as Tony Stark / Iron Man

Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a genius billionaire and the creator of the high-tech Iron Man suit. As a founding member of the Avengers in the Marvel Universe.

Captain America

Captain America, also known as , is a superhero created by Joe Simon and Jack Kirby.
He first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941. As a soldier during World War II, Steve Rogers was given the Super Soldier Serum, transforming him into the iconic hero with enhanced strength, agility, and endurance. His shield, made from vibranium, is his signature weapon, known for its ability to absorb impacts and return to him when thrown. Over the years, Captain America has been a symbol of justice and freedom, representing the best of humanity.

Thor

Chris Hemsworth as Thor
Thor, the God of Thunder, is one of the most powerful and revered figures in Asgardian mythology. Created by Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, and Larry Lieber, Thor first appeared in Journey into Mystery #83 in 1962. Wielding the enchanted hammer Mjölnir, Thor possesses the ability to control thunder and lightning. As the son of Odin, the Allfather, Thor is also a member of the Avengers, fighting to protect both Earth and the Nine Realms from powerful threats. Thor’s strength, honor, and his duty as protector have made him a hero in both the mortal and divine worlds.

Spider-Man

Tom Holland as Spider-Man

Spider-Man, the friendly neighborhood hero, gained his powers from a radioactive spider bite. Balancing his everyday life with heroic deeds, he swings through New York City using his web-slinging abilities.

Deadpool

Ryan Reynolds as Deadpool

Deadpool, known as the Merc with a Mouth, is famous for his irreverent humor and tendency to break the fourth wall. His unorthodox methods and unpredictable nature make him a standout antihero in the Marvel Universe.

Loki

Tom Hiddleston as Loki

Loki, the God of Mischief, is known for his cunning, charm, and ever-changing loyalties. His complex personality and knack for misdirection make him one of the most intriguing characters in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.

Doctor Strange

Benedict Cumberbatch as Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange, the Sorcerer Supreme, defends Earth from mystical threats using his mastery of the occult. His journey from a brilliant neurosurgeon to a powerful magician has redefined what it means to be a superhero.

Hulk

Mark Ruffalo as Hulk

Hulk, the alter ego of Dr. Bruce Banner, is a symbol of uncontrollable power and raw strength. When pushed to his limits, Banner transforms into the green behemoth known for his immense physical might and indomitable spirit.

Scarlet Witch

Elizabeth Olsen as Scarlet Witch

Scarlet Witch, with her reality-altering powers, plays a pivotal role in the Marvel Universe. Her journey of loss, power, and redemption adds a rich emotional layer to the complex narrative of the Avengers.

Black Widow

Scarlett Johansson as Black Widow

Black Widow is a master spy with unparalleled combat skills and a mysterious past. Her dedication to protecting the world, even at great personal cost, makes her an indispensable member of the Avengers.
