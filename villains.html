Marvel Villains

Thanos

Josh Brolin as Thanos

Thanos, the Mad Titan, seeks to bring balance to the universe by collecting the Infinity Stones.

Loki

Tom Hiddleston as Loki

The God of Mischief, Loki constantly shifts between villain and anti-hero in his quest for power.

Ultron

James Spader as Ultron

Created by Tony Stark, Ultron is an AI with a god complex who seeks to eradicate humanity.

Green Goblin

Willem Dafoe as Green Goblin

Norman Osborn's alter ego, the Green Goblin, is Spider-Man’s arch-nemesis.

Doctor Doom

Victor Von Doom

A genius scientist and sorcerer, Doctor Doom is a major foe of the Fantastic Four.

Magneto

Ian McKellen / Michael Fassbender as Magneto

The master of magnetism and leader of the Brotherhood of Mutants.

Red Skull

Hugo Weaving as Red Skull

The former leader of Hydra and archenemy of Captain America.

Venom

Tom Hardy as Venom

A symbiote-driven anti-hero who began as one of Spider-Man’s greatest foes.

Carnage

Woody Harrelson as Carnage

A psychotic symbiote even more dangerous than Venom.

Kingpin

Vincent D’Onofrio as Kingpin

A ruthless crime lord controlling New York City.

Hela

Cate Blanchett as Hela

The Goddess of Death and Thor’s powerful sister.

Mysterio

Jake Gyllenhaal as Mysterio

A master illusionist and enemy of Spider-Man.

Killmonger

Michael B. Jordan as Killmonger

A skilled warrior with a deep hatred for Wakanda’s monarchy.
